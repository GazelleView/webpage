Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

At Gazelle Code, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website luxenest.com, make a purchase, subscribe to our newsletter, or otherwise interact with our services. By using our site, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, as well as information collected automatically when you browse our site. This may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment details when you place an project or create an account.
  • Browsing Data: IP address, browser type, device information, referring URLs, pages visited, and time spent on each page via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Purchase History: Projects you've viewed, added to cart, or purchased, along with project frequency and preferences.
  • Communication Data: Records of correspondence when you contact our customer service team, including email threads and chat logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us deliver a seamless, personalized luxury shopping experience. We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Project Fulfillment: Processing payments, shipping projects, sending project confirmations, and managing returns or exchanges.
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries, troubleshooting issues, and providing tailored design advice.
  • Marketing & Newsletter: Sending curated emails with design inspiration, new arrivals, and exclusive offers (only with your explicit consent).
  • Site Improvement: Analyzing user behavior to enhance navigation, project recommendations, and overall experience.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting tax obligations, fraud prevention, and responding to lawful requests from authorities.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Gazelle Code uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember your preferences, keep items in your shopping bag, and understand how you interact with our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser.

Types of cookies we use:

Essential: Necessary for core functionality — cart, checkout, and account login.
Analytics: Helps us understand traffic patterns and popular projects.
Marketing: Delivers relevant ads and measures campaign performance.
Preferences: Remembers language, currency, and wishlist items.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect site functionality and your shopping experience.

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted partners who help us operate our business, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal — to securely handle transactions.
  • Shipping Carriers: FedEx, DHL, UPS — to deliver your projects.
  • Email & Analytics: Klaviyo, Google Analytics — for newsletter delivery and site insights.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, court project, or to protect our rights and property.

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely for the purposes we specify.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing — we never store full credit card numbers.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Strict internal access controls — only authorized personnel can access personal data.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to processing for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@luxenest.com. We will respond within 30 calendar days. Verification of identity may be required.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Typically:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, plus 24 months after last activity.
  • Project records: Retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations.
  • Marketing consent: Retained indefinitely unless you withdraw consent.

8. Children's Privacy

Gazelle Code is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will promptly delete it from our records. If you believe a child has shared information with us, please contact us immediately at privacy@luxenest.com.

9. International Data Transfers

Gazelle Code is headquartered in the United States. If you are located outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed on servers in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure that appropriate safeguards — such as Standard Contractual Clauses — are in place to protect your data during such transfers, in compliance with applicable data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or new features. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this page regularly. For material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website at least 14 days before the changes take effect.
