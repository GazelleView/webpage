How project updates work

Once your project is packed and leaves our studio, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link. Here's what to expect:

  • In‑stock items ship within 1–3 business days. Custom and made‑to‑project pieces ship in 4–6 weeks.
  • Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours after you receive your email to activate.
  • If your project contains multiple items, each may ship separately and have its own tracking number.
  • For international projects, tracking may pause at customs — this is normal and typically resolves within 2–3 days.

Need a project update?

If you don't have your tracking number, can't log in, or see an unexpected status — our team will help you get answers within one business day.
