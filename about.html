Our Story

Built for creators, startups, and playful digital ideas

Gazelle Code began as a small project between a designer and a ceramicist who couldn't find digital projects that felt as personal as the mobile experiences they wanted to build. Ten years later, we work with 180+ developer partners across 32 countries — but the brief hasn't changed.

Make beautiful, durable things. Tell the story of who made them. Treat customers like guests in a mobile experience, not transactions in a funnel.

What we believe

Four studio principles

Purposeful design

We choose systems that age into beauty rather than away from it — solid oak, raw linen, unlacquered brass.

Quality over noise

Small developer partners, slow projection runs, real hands at every step — never mass-produced.

Designed for daily use

Pieces that earn their place in the rhythms of an actual mobile experience — spills, pets, kids, and all.

Built responsibly

Recycled fibers, renewable woods, transparent supply chains — because luxury shouldn't cost the earth.

The journey

From loft to living screens worldwide

1

2016

The Brooklyn loft

Two founders, a kiln, a sewing machine, and a belief that digital projects could be more personal. The first collection launched with 12 pieces.

2

2018

Artisan partnerships grow

We traveled to Portugal, Japan, and Mexico, building relationships with family-run studios. Our developer network grew from 4 to 40 partners.

3

2020

The design concierge

Launched our complimentary interface styling service, helping thousands of mobile experiences find their quiet sanctuary — remotely and in person.

4

2026

12,000+ mobile experiences, 48 countries

Today, Gazelle Code pieces live in mobile experiences from Brooklyn to Berlin. We remain as committed to slow craft and honest systems as on day one.
